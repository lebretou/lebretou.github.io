About me

I am a first-year computer science master's student at Brown University. In my senior year at Emory University, I worked with Dr. Emily Wall, who leads Emory CAV Lab. My research interests are data visualization and human-computer interaction.

In my most recent project, I replicated Cleveland and McGill's graphical perception study and expanded their method to the tactile modality. We evaluated elementary data encoding types on tactile paper, which is a commonly-used medium for people who are blind or with low vision.
